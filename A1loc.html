Workshop on local A1-Brouwer degree

20-24 January 2022, online workshop

The main purpose of this workshop is to study a theorem of Kass-Wickelgren which identifies the local A1-Brouwer degree with the Eisenbud–Khimshiashvili–Levine class in the Grotheodieck-Witt ring.
