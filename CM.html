Workshop on condensed mathematics

28-31 July 2023, Tongji University

In this workshop, we aim to study the theory of condensed mathematics developed by Clausen-Scholze.

This workshop is supported by the National Key Research and Development Program of China Grant Nr.2021YFA1001400, the NSFC Grant Nr.12101455 and the Fundamental Research Funds for the Central Universities.
