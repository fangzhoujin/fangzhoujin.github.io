Workshop on vanishing cycles and Swan conductors

2023.05.20-2023.05.21, Peking University
Room 1303, Sciences Building No.1 北京大学理科1号楼1303

In this workshop, we aim to study vanishing cycles/topos, local Fourier transform and Gabber-Katz extension in etale cohomology.
